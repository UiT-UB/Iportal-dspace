Submission help - doctoral theses

Submit: Describe your thesis

On availability in Munin:

Here you may give information if you want (parts of) your thesis not to be made openly available in Munin for a period. Be exact and explain which parts this applies to, and for how long. Be aware that the PhD regulations (Section 21 (5)) reads as follows:

"Published parts of the thesis shall, as a main rule, be openly available either in open access journals or in UiT's access archive. Unpublished parts of the thesis shall be openly available no later than two years after the defence and unpublished monographs no later than four years after the defence. All parts of a thesis must be openly available no later than five years after the defence."

Here you also may place questions on the issue of open access to your thesis, and those handling this will contact you.

Name of candidate:

Your name is transferred automatically from the student registry. If you find that it is misspelled, you may correct it.

Title:

Enter the full title of your thesis.

Abstract:

Please enter (or paste) abstract(s) of your thesis, to be included in Munin together with your thesis. We recommend an abstract of 200-300 word. The abstract helps readers to quickly understand what your thesis is about, and it improves search and retrieval of your thesis. You must enter the abstract in both English and Norwegian. You may also enter abstracts in other languages.

Approved instruction component:

Please indicate if the instruction component is approved.

Keywords:

You may enter free text keywords which describe the content of your thesis. Good keywords improve the retrieval of your thesis. Enter one keyword only, or one phrase, per box. Click "Add More" to enter more keywords.

Language:

Choose the language in which the thesis is written. If the language is missing in the list, chose "(Other)".

Click "Next" to proceed. Or you may click "Cancel/Save", and save your submission. You may then log in and continue later, and nothing of your entered information will be lost.

Submit: Here you may upload the following

File:

Click "Select a file" and you your folders and files are shown in a window. Find the correct file.

File Description:

Please explain carefully the content of the file. The description is especially important if you upload more than one file, in order for the administration to clearly see which file contains what.

Click Next to upload the file. You may upload one file only at a time.

Uploaded files

For each uploaded file, please make sure that you have uploaded the correct one. Click the file name to open the file, and make sure that it is the correct one.

In order to upload more than one file, click "Add Another File".

When you have uploaded all files, click "Next".

Submit: Verify Submission

Please proofread the information you have entered in your submission. You may correct this by clicking the appropriate button to the right. When you have made sure everything is fine, please click "Next" to proceed.

You may still click "Cancel/Save", and save your submission. You will then need to log in and finalize your submission later. Nothing of your entered information will be lost.

Submit: License agreement

Please read the license agreement closely. If you have any questions on this, please contact Munin at munin@ub.uit.no.

Submit: Submission Complete

As soon as your thesis is submitted, the administration at your faculty will forward it to the committee. Your thesis will be made available in Munin after it is approved for defence, with consideration, however, to to any wishes you have expressed regarding delaying open access to (parts of) your thesis. As soon as the thesis is made available in Munin, a message will be sent to your UiT e-mail account.

More help

If you need further help or have any other questions, you may contact the study administration at your faculty.

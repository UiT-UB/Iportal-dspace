Checklist for documents to be included
in Munin open research archive

 Print  Close

In order to include your document in Munin, some legal and ethical conditions must be fulfilled. The author, together with the institution, is responsible for the content of the document.

For your assistance, here is a short checklist with questions. If you answer "yes" to any of the questions, the document may not be included in Munin. If you are in doubt, please contact your supervisor, your department or the Munin administrator.

Questions regarding legal and ethical aspects related to documents to be included in Munin open research archive

Does your thesis contain personal or confidential information on named individuals or individuals that are easy to identify? Yes No
Does your thesis contain information that was given to you from individuals where you made a declaration of non-disclosure of confidential information? Yes No
Does your thesis contain confidential information from a public administration? Yes No
Does your document contain material or information that is protected by copyright, and for which the copyright holder has not consented in the use of the material in an electronic version of your document? Yes No

If you answer "no" to all the questions above, your document may be included in Munin.

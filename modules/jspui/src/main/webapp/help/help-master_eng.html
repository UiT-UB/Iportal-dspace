Submission help - master theses

Submit: Describe your thesis

Author:

Your name is collected automatically from the student registry as you log in. If you find that it is misspelled, you may correct it. Please enter the names of your co-authors, if two or more students have written a cooperative thesis.

Title:

Enter the full title of your thesis.

Abstract:

Please enter (or paste) abstract(s) of your thesis, to be included in Munin together with your thesis. We recommend an abstract of 200-300 words. The abstract helps readers to quickly see what your thesis is about, and improves search and retrieval of your thesis. Click "Add More" to enter abstracts in more than one language. You may enter abstract(s) in any and as many languages as you prefer.

Subject keywords:

You may enter free text keywords which describe the content of your thesis. Good keywords improve the retrieval of your thesis. Enter one keyword only, or one phrase, per box. Click "Add More" to enter more keywords.

Language:

Choose the language in which the thesis is written. If the language is missing in the list, chose "(Other)".

Click "Next" to proceed. Or you may click "Cancel/Save", and save your submission. You may log in and continue later, and nothing of your entered information will be lost.

Submit: Further information

Messages:

Please enter any messages you wish to pass on to the administration at your faculty, concerning your submission and/or your thesis.

Submit: Upload files

File:

Click "Browse" ("Bla gjennom") and your folders and files are shown in a window. Find the correct file, and upload it.

File description:

Please explain carefully the content of the file. The description is especially important if you upload more than one file, in order for the administration to clearly see which file contains what.

Uploaded file:

For each uploaded file, please make sure that you have uploaded the correct one. Click the file name to open the file, and make sure that it is the correct one.

In order to upload more than one file, click "Next". Then you may click "Add Another File".

When you have uploaded all files, click "Next".

Submit: Verify submission

Please proofread the information you have entered in your submission. You may correct this by clicking the appropriate button to the right. When you have made sure everything is fine, please click "Next" to proceed.

You may still click "Cancel/Save", and save your submission. You will then need to log in and finalize your submission later. Nothing of your entered information will be lost.

Submit: License agreement

If you want your thesis available in Munin, you need to accept the license agreement. Please read the license agreement closely. If you have any questions on this, please contact Munin on munin@ub.uit.no.

If you have chosen not to have your thesis included in Munin, you will not see any license agreement.

Submit: Submission complete

As soon as your thesis is submitted, the administration at your department or faculty will deal with it, and forward it to the examining committee and to the printing office. Your thesis will be made available in Munin after it is approved, unless you have stated otherwise in the submission process. As soon as the thesis is made available in Munin, a message will be sent to the same address this e-mail is sent to.

More help

If you need further help or have any other questions, you may contact the study administration at your department/faculty.
